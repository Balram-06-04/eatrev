About Taste Radar

Welcome to Taste Radar – your one-stop platform to discover, share, and review food stalls around you. Our mission is simple: connect food lovers with hidden gems and help small vendors reach more people.

Started in 2025, we are a passionate team driven by innovation, design, and love for food. Together, we aim to make eating out more fun, transparent, and rewarding.

What We Do

Vendor Booking

Vendors can register and get bookings directly through TasteRadar. Customers can easily hire food stalls for events and functions.

Street Food Reviews

Discover authentic reviews of local street food stalls. Share your own experience and help others find the best places to eat.

Health Matters

We know street food is tasty, but sometimes unhealthy or unhygienic. TasteRadar helps you pick safe and trusted stalls with real reviews.

“We’ve all been there – enjoying delicious street food but later regretting it due to hygiene. At TasteRadar, we want to make sure you can enjoy taste without the risk.”
